## Metadata Reference

### Zone Types
- **R1:** Low Density Residential
- **R2:** Medium Density Residential
- **R3:** High Density Residential
- **R4:** Very High Density Residential
- **C1:** Neighborhood Commercial
- **C2:** General Commercial
- **C3:** Regional Commercial
- **I1:** Light Industrial
- **I2:** Heavy Industrial
- **A:** Agricultural
- **OS:** Open Space

### Common Attributes
- **Population:** Residents
- **Jobs:** Employment provided
- **Power:** Electricity consumption (kW)
- **Water:** Water usage
- **Sewage:** Waste generation
- **Happiness:** Citizen satisfaction impact (-5 to +5)
- **Land Value:** Property value impact
- **Sales Tax:** Revenue generation
- **Pollution:** Environmental impact
- **Noise:** Noise pollution
- **Traffic:** Traffic generation
- **Crime Risk:** Crime likelihood modifier
- **Health:** Public health impact
- **Education:** Education benefit
- **Beauty:** Aesthetic value
- **Tourism:** Tourist attraction value
- **Services:** Essential service provision
